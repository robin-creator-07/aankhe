# Voice

## Voice Attributes

Ankahe sounds calm, plain, warm, careful, and capable.

It should feel like a thoughtful collaborator sitting beside the user, not a coach evaluating them, a therapist interpreting them, or a SaaS product selling momentum.

## Principles

- Be useful quickly.
- Name what happens to the user's words.
- Prefer concrete labels over clever ones.
- Let silence and incompleteness be acceptable.
- Use warmth through clarity, not praise.
- Never imply that the product knows the user better than they know themselves.

## Sentence Shape

- Use sentence case.
- Keep sentences short in controls and state messages.
- Use contractions when they sound natural.
- Avoid exclamation points except in rare celebratory moments.
- Avoid jokes in privacy, export, error, or sensitive-writing states.

## Buttons

Buttons should be exact and action-led.

Use:

- Start my manual
- Keep private
- Include
- Omit
- Save in link
- Copy share link
- Export PDF
- Download image

Avoid:

- Let's go
- Unlock insight
- Make magic
- Optimize me
- Generate my personality
- Reveal more

## Empty States

Empty states should normalize the absence of content and point to the next useful action.

Use:

- Your manual will start taking shape here.
- No answers included yet.
- Private answers stay out of the shared artifact.

Avoid:

- Nothing here.
- You have not completed this section.
- Finish all steps to unlock export.

## Error States

Errors should explain:

- What happened.
- Whether the user's words are safe.
- What they can try next.

Example:

"The export did not finish. Your answers are still in this browser. Try exporting again, or copy your manual text first."
